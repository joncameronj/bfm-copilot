# BFM Master Protocol Key: Contraindications & Safety Warnings

CRITICAL SAFETY INFORMATION: These contraindications must be checked before
ANY frequency protocol is recommended. Failure to screen for these conditions
could result in adverse events.

---

## Contraindication: Pregnant

**Risk:** Safest to avoid all frequency technology

**Avoid:** All frequencies

**Alternative / Solution:** Can run frequencies through water instead

---

## Contraindication: Pacemaker

**Risk:** Can interfere with device

**Avoid:** Direct frequency application

**Alternative / Solution:** Run frequencies through water as alternative

---

## Contraindication: Cardiac Monitor

**Risk:** Will skew readings; confuse doctors

**Avoid:** Direct frequency on chest area

**Alternative / Solution:** Inform cardiologist; use water method

---

## Contraindication: Stents

**Risk:** Avoid electron generator; careful with high-power PEMF

**Avoid:** High-power settings away from stent

**Alternative / Solution:** Lower power settings may be acceptable

---

## Contraindication: Insulin Pump

**Risk:** Can shut off device

**Avoid:** Direct frequency away from pump

**Alternative / Solution:** Patient can remove pump temporarily if able

---

## Contraindication: Pain Pump

**Risk:** Can shut off device

**Avoid:** Direct frequency away from pump

**Alternative / Solution:** Patient can remove temporarily if able

---

## Contraindication: Dehydrated

**Risk:** Frequencies won't work well; need conductivity

**Avoid:** All frequencies less effective

**Alternative / Solution:** Hydrate patient before treatment

---

## Contraindication: Low DHA/Iodine

**Risk:** Poor technology tolerance; shocks easily

**Avoid:** Patient may not tolerate current

**Alternative / Solution:** Supplement DHA/Iodine first; start low

---

## Contraindication: Constipation + Foot Bath

**Risk:** Constipation setting causes urgent bowel movement

**Avoid:** Constipation freq with foot bath

**Alternative / Solution:** Ensure bathroom access; warn patient

---

## Contraindication: Stenosis + CTF

**Risk:** Cervical Trauma Fibro may antagonize stenosis

**Avoid:** CTF-P frequency

**Alternative / Solution:** Use CTF-A (alternating version) instead

---

## Contraindication: High ferritin — NEVER give iron

**Risk:** Creates hydroxyl radicals; extremely dangerous

**Avoid:** Iron supplementation, high iron foods

**Alternative / Solution:** Use IP6 Gold to chelate; CoQ10 for protection

---

## Contraindication: Over-antioxidant (PAT >3400)

**Risk:** Crushing oxidative phosphorylation

**Avoid:** More antioxidants

**Alternative / Solution:** Reduce antioxidant supplementation
