## Introduction: What is Energetic Debt? (0:00 - 1:50)

**[Watch this section](https://whop.com/joined/beyond-functional-medicine/exp_ZLhOFH8OcvlPRV/app/courses/cors_LstpN0QzwLAaf/lessons/lesn_IlrsKvXYFOMOq/?t=7)**

The body really ultimately needs two things:

If it doesn't have either of those two things (or both together), you're going to end up in an emergency situation and the body is going to panic.

## The Business Analogy (1:50 - 3:30)

**[Watch this section](https://whop.com/joined/beyond-functional-medicine/exp_ZLhOFH8OcvlPRV/app/courses/cors_LstpN0QzwLAaf/lessons/lesn_IlrsKvXYFOMOq/?t=110)**

Think of energetic debt like owning a business:
- Every business **spends money** and **makes money**
- Hopefully it makes more than it spends
- When you spend more than you make, you're in a debt cycle

It's the exact same thing inside the body, except **instead of money, it's energy**.

When you're not making as much energy as you need to run all the things inside your "business," your **product starts to suffer**.

### What's Your Product?

Your product is **healthy cells**. The goal of the human body is to have cells that perform functions, and when they're old and done, they need to be replaced by new ones. The better and more efficient you are at replacing those cells, the healthier you are and the longer you last.

That depends upon the amount of energy creation inside the body.

## Your Immune System: Quality Control (3:30 - 6:30)

**[Watch this section](https://whop.com/joined/beyond-functional-medicine/exp_ZLhOFH8OcvlPRV/app/courses/cors_LstpN0QzwLAaf/lessons/lesn_IlrsKvXYFOMOq/?t=210)**

In energetic debt, cells come off the assembly line that don't look right, don't function correctly, and don't communicate properly. Now you have a group of mutated cells.

Luckily, before these cells come off your product line, you have a slip and check mechanism: **your immune system**.

### What Does Your Immune System Really Do?

Most people think the immune system is constantly fighting infections and invaders. But **90% of what your immune system does is quality control** of your cells that your body makes.

You have about **37 trillion cells** in your body and will replace **hundreds of thousands of cells a day**. Your immune system is like an elegant janitorial service making sure these cells are correct.

### The Car Accident Analogy

If your immune system finds a cell that's not correct, it has two choices:

1. **Repair it** - "This isn't too bad. It's minor damage. It pays to spend the energy to fix this problem."
2. **Replace it** - "This one's totaled. No sense in putting energy into this. Just get rid of it and get a brand new one."

### When the System Gets Overwhelmed

If energy loss gets so great that too many damaged cells are coming off the assembly line, it will eventually overwhelm the immune system. The immune system starts making concessions: "This cell's not awesome, but we're going to use it. This is better than nothing."

The other option is closing the factory and shutting down - which means you die. So the body says, "Let's hope the economy turns and we start making more energy. For right now, we'll do what we need to do to stay open and buy as much time as possible."
