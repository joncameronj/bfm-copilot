## Lever 1: Melatonin (2:00 - 11:30)

**[Watch this section](https://whop.com/joined/beyond-functional-medicine/exp_ZLhOFH8OcvlPRV/app/courses/cors_LstpN0QzwLAaf/lessons/lesn_ePL6rnbhsYSv4/?t=120)**

### Melatonin is NOT Just a Sleep Supplement

Would you be surprised if I told you that **melatonin is the single most important hormone inside your entire body**?

Melatonin controls your body's ability to perform:
- **Autophagy** - repairing DNA damage
- **Apoptosis** - replacing cells that are "totaled"
- **Directly improving heteroplasmic rates**

Your body should make melatonin **every single day** to keep up with constant repair work. The goal is to stay caught up - like doing paperwork every night instead of letting it pile up until you're scrambling.

### Where is Melatonin Made?

- **5%** from your pineal gland (what most people know)
- **95%** made directly in your mitochondria (new research)

It's directly involved in the repair and replacement process your immune system goes through.

### How to Make Melatonin (Don't Supplement It)

**Your body makes melatonin through exposure to light AND darkness:**

1. **Morning light** (before tanning angle) - stimulates cells to START making melatonin
2. **UV light** (tanning light) - CHARGES the melatonin so it's ready to work
3. **After 4 hours of perceived darkness** - melatonin starts being secreted and used

**Key insight**: Sleep without melatonin means you'll rest, but you will NOT repair and regenerate your body.

### The 4-Hour Rule

Your body needs **4 hours of perceived darkness** before melatonin starts doing its work.

**How It Should Work:**
- Sun goes down → darkness triggers the 4-hour clock
- By the time you go to bed → melatonin cycles begin immediately
- 8 hours of melatonin-regenerating sleep

**How It Works for Most People Today:**
- Look at phone right before bed → 4-hour clock starts THEN
- Sleep 6 hours → only 2 hours of actual regeneration
- That's **25%** of what you need

This is why **alien light at night (ALAN)** is one of the most dangerous things for human health.

### Why NOT to Take Melatonin Supplements

1. **Throws off biofeedback loops** - your body stops making its own
2. **Can tag wrong things** for your immune system to attack or ignore
3. **Damages your eyes** - melatonin puts your body in "eye dark mode"; light then rips through your retina

Two research papers already prove that taking melatonin while exposed to light causes retinal damage.

> **"Earn it. Don't cheat."**

## Lever 2: Leptin (11:30 - 18:30)

**[Watch this section](https://whop.com/joined/beyond-functional-medicine/exp_ZLhOFH8OcvlPRV/app/courses/cors_LstpN0QzwLAaf/lessons/lesn_ePL6rnbhsYSv4/?t=690)**

### Leptin is Your Energy Accountant

Leptin operates like your **energy or electron accountant**. It:
- Tells your body where to put energy
- Controls metabolism
- Signals your brain about the financial status of your "business"

### How Leptin Works

Leptin is made in your **fat cells** and signals your brain:

**When leptin works correctly:**
- Brain knows how much energy is available
- Excess energy gets burned off as heat
- "Fast metabolism" - keeps weight off, runs hot

**When leptin resistant:**
- Accountant can't tell CEO (brain) how much energy exists
- Body stores everything (even excess energy)
- Can't lose weight no matter how hard you try

### The Melatonin-Leptin Connection

**Here's the order of hormones in the circadian cycle:**

1. **Pineal gland** releases melatonin (after 4 hours of darkness)
2. **Melatonin binds at hypothalamus** → lowers body temperature
3. **Fat tissue secretes leptin** → binds at hypothalamus
4. **Optimal time: midnight to 2:00 AM**

**If you miss this window** (swing shifts, late nights, looking at phone before bed), the cascade breaks.

### What Leptin Controls

Once melatonin and leptin signal correctly:
- **Pancreas** → lowers insulin (prevents diabetes)
- **Thyroid** → upregulates function
- **Pituitary** → secretes prolactin, growth hormone, MSH

**If leptin can't use energy:**
- Stores it in liver → fatty liver
- Metabolic syndrome
- Diabetes cascade

> **Light and darkness exposure controls your weight even more than food.**

## Lever 3: Vitamin D (18:30 - 23:30)

**[Watch this section](https://whop.com/joined/beyond-functional-medicine/exp_ZLhOFH8OcvlPRV/app/courses/cors_LstpN0QzwLAaf/lessons/lesn_ePL6rnbhsYSv4/?t=1110)**

### Vitamin D is Actually a Hormone

Vitamin D is not a vitamin - it's a **pro-hormone**. They just found it between C and E, so they called it vitamin D.

### What Vitamin D Does

Vitamin D is a **global effector** - there's pretty much no disease you can Google without finding vitamin D plays a major role.

**Vitamin D regulates over 900 different genes by itself** - that's **3% of our entire genome**.

### How Vitamin D is Made

Created when the sun reaches the angle that produces **UV light** (the type that creates tan/color on skin).

**Key facts:**
- There is no food source without sunlight
- There is no life on Earth without sunlight
- The more sun you get, the less ALL-cause mortality (including skin cancers!)
- The #1 finding in people with skin cancers: **low vitamin D**

### The Vitamin D - Leptin Connection

These are linked in a specific ratio:
- As **vitamin D goes up**, leptin comes down
- As **leptin goes up**, vitamin D comes down

**Low sun exposure = low vitamin D = leptin resistance = weight gain**

If you want to lose weight: get outside in the sun.

### Why Not to Just Take Vitamin D Supplements

When you take vitamin D in pill form:
- Out of 20-30 functions vitamin D serves, you only get **1-2**
- It looks nice on paper but you don't get the benefits
- You won't understand your true light absorption capacity

> **"You can't pay somebody to do push-ups for you."**

### What is MSH?

MSH = Melanocyte Stimulating Hormone
- Melanocytes are the pigment in your skin that give it color
- Darker skin = more melanin = typically more MSH
- Made from sun exposure (similar spectrum to vitamin D)

### MSH and Chronic Disease

**MSH is catastrophically low in at least 70% of people with severe chronic diseases** - often so low it can't even be measured.

There is:
- No pill for MSH
- No hormone replacement for MSH
- No supplement form

It has to be made from your body's exposure to sunlight.

### What MSH Does

MSH directly:
- **Lowers heteroplasmy rates**
- **Repairs DNA damage**
- **Stimulates endorphins** (stable energy)
- **Boosts immune function**
- **Is anti-inflammatory**
- **Keeps everything tight** (prevents leaky gut)
- **Reduces gluten sensitivity**
- **Protects against autoimmune diseases**
- **Protects blood sugar, thyroid, vascular system**

### The MSH-Melatonin Feedback Loop

The more MSH you make, the more it **cycles back to stimulate your body to make more melatonin**. They feed each other.

### What Destroys MSH

- **Blue light at night** (indoor lighting, screens, LED bulbs)
- **Non-native EMF** (cell phones, Wi-Fi)

If leptin is incorrect (leptin resistant), **MSH won't work**.

Most neurodegenerative disorders are **protein misfolding disorders**:
- Protein supposed to fold one way
- Misfolds by accident
- Immune system recognizes it doesn't look right
- Tries to fix it or get rid of it

When rushing (energetically behind), things get missed - that's how these disorders develop.
