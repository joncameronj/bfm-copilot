## How Mitochondria Actually Work (0:00 - 2:00)

**[Watch this section](https://whop.com/joined/beyond-functional-medicine/exp_ZLhOFH8OcvlPRV/app/courses/cors_LstpN0QzwLAaf/lessons/lesn_u8hj9XrqvuerQ/?t=7)**

Think of mitochondria like an appliance plugged into an electrical outlet:
- **Plug in** → receive electrons
- **Electrical cord** → voltage charge
- **End of cord** → mitochondria **spins** to make energy

**The faster it spins, the more energy it creates.**

Your mitochondria spin at **9,000 rotations per minute** (about 300 rotations per second). This is significantly faster than a car engine.

### What Goes In and What Comes Out

**Input:**
- Electrons + Oxygen

**Output:**
- Carbon dioxide (CO2)
- ATP (usable energy)
- Water (special metabolic water)

## Exclusion Zone (EZ) Water: Your Internal Battery (2:00 - 4:30)

**[Watch this section](https://whop.com/joined/beyond-functional-medicine/exp_ZLhOFH8OcvlPRV/app/courses/cors_LstpN0QzwLAaf/lessons/lesn_u8hj9XrqvuerQ/?t=120)**

The water your mitochondria produce is not regular tap water. This is **exclusion zone water** - the fourth phase of water.

### What is EZ Water?

- Not gas, not liquid, not solid
- Somewhere between liquid and solid
- Flexible but crystalline structure
- Under a microscope: incredibly organized

### What EZ Water Does

1. **Acts as a battery** - stores energy
2. **Insulates the charge** - remember the 30 million volt charge across mitochondrial membranes
3. **Prevents electrocution** - insulates so we don't lose electricity

**If this water decreases, energy production crashes and the battery dies.**

### Your Body Makes More Water Than You Drink

- Typical recommendation: drink 2-3 liters per day
- **Your body makes about 4.5 liters of metabolic water per day**

Nobody talks about this. This is why camels survive in the desert - they're significantly better at creating water using sun than we are.

### Why "Dehydration" Isn't Always About Drinking More

When patients say: "Doc, I drink so much water I'm going to float away, but my lab work shows dehydration..."

**The problem isn't water intake - it's mitochondrial water production.**

Signs of dehydration on lab work typically mean your mitochondria aren't spinning properly to make enough EZ water.

### Hydrogen: The Fuel for Mitochondrial Spin

The human body chose **hydrogen** as the fuel that makes mitochondria spin. Why?

Hydrogen is in the corner of the periodic table because it's the **lightest element**. If something works by spinning fast, you want the lightest possible fuel.

### Three Types of Hydrogen

| Type | Also Called | Weight | What Happens |
|------|-------------|--------|--------------|
| **Protium** | Light hydrogen | Lightest | Mitochondria spin fast - WANT THIS |
| **Deuterium** | Heavy hydrogen | 2x weight | Gunks up engines, slows spin - AVOID |
| Tritium | - | Heaviest | Not found in human body |

### Why Deuterium is a Problem

**Deuterium is DOUBLE the molecular weight of protium.**

Think about lifting weights: if I can curl a certain amount but you double the weight, I can't:
- Curl as fast
- Curl as many times

When deuterium gets into mitochondria:
- Engines don't spin as fast
- They get "gunked up"
- Energy production drops significantly

### Deuterium Skips Repair, Goes Straight to Replace

Remember autophagy (repair) vs apoptosis (replace)?

**Normal process:** Insurance adjuster looks at scratch on bumper → "Easy repair, spend the energy to fix it."

**With excess deuterium:** Insurance adjuster looks at scratch on bumper → "Total the car."

This is not a reasonable response, but deuterium causes the body to:
- Skip autophagy (repair)
- Jump immediately to apoptosis (replacement)

### Why This Leads to Disease

**Neurodegeneration:**
- Nervous system cells don't replace themselves fast
- You run out of stem cells
- Results in serious neurodegenerative disorders

**Cancer:**
- Cells can only replace themselves a certain number of times (say, 20)
- If spread over 100 years → you last 100 years
- If deuterium burns through replacements in 50 years → cells hit retirement age early
- Cell says: "My job's not done but I'm exhausted. I'll either do nothing OR break all the rules to contribute."
- **Breaking the rules to survive = cancer**

Deuterium makes you reach that point significantly faster.

### High-Sun Foods Contain More Deuterium

Deuterium naturally occurs in:
- Many fruits
- Many vegetables
- Foods grown in high sunlight

### The Confusion Problem

When you eat a food grown in high sunlight, **your body assumes YOU are in high sunlight**.

**Scenario:**
- You live somewhere with high sun
- You work indoors all day (low sun exposure)
- You eat foods grown in high sun
- Food = high sun, Body = low sun
- **Result: Confusion → Inflammation**

Even if it's a "healthy" food, it will create inflammation if there's a mismatch.

### Why Carnivore/Keto Works for Some People

Part of why people get better on carnivore or keto diets: **they accidentally deplete deuterium** from their system pretty aggressively - even though they don't realize that's what they're doing.

### People Who Eat High-Deuterium Foods Without Problems

"I eat lots of those foods and don't have high deuterium. Why?"

They get a lot of sun. Their five levers are correct from sun exposure, which keeps deuterium away from mitochondria where it doesn't belong.
