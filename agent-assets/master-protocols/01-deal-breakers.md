These are the 7 critical deal breakers that MUST be addressed before proceeding
with any frequency protocol. If a patient presents with any of these findings,
the corresponding protocol takes absolute priority.

**Diagnostic Finding:** HRV: Red dot crosses to wrong side of blue dot (right instead of left)

**Indicates:** Emotional trauma, PTSD, wires crossed

**Frequency Protocol:** SNS Balance (35 at 709)

**Supplementation:** None specific

**Source Module(s):** All 4 modules

**Timeline:** Weeks–months
