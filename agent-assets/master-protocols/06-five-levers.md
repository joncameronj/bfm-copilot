*Also called the Five Archimedes Levers, 5 Archimedes Levers, or simply "the levers" — named after Archimedes' principle: "Give me a lever and a place to stand and I'll move the world." These are Dr. Rob DeMartino's highest-leverage intervention points in BFM methodology.*

The 5 master levers that govern metabolic health in the BFM framework.
These are the foundational markers that must be assessed and optimized:
Melatonin, Leptin, MSH, Vitamin D, and UB Rates.

## Master Lever: 1. Melatonin

**How to Assess:** Saliva test 2 AM (Genova, $120); sleep quality assessment; alpha/theta ratio

**Problem Indicators:** Low sleep quality; circadian disruption; alpha/theta reversed

**Frequency Protocol:** Pineal Support; Adrenal Quiet (at night)

**Supplementation / Interventions:** Epi Pineal (Innovita — no exogenous melatonin)
Silent Night Patch (50% get boost if methylator)
Blue blocking glasses by 8 PM

## Master Lever: 3. MSH

**How to Assess:** Lab test ($80); unmeasurable in 60-70% MS patients

**Problem Indicators:** Low MSH <8-40; can't stimulate endorphins; pain; fatigue.  Under 15 patient will lose regenerative sleep; healthy range 20-25.

**Frequency Protocol:** Pit A Support, Pars Intermedia

**Supplementation / Interventions:** Hypothala (Innovita — has Hypothalamus + pituitary codes)
Sunlight (primary treatment)
Apex nasal spray (if medication-resistant staph blocking MSH)

**Source:** All 4

## Master Lever: 4. Vitamin D

**How to Assess:** Blood test (target 60-80); D-Minder app; Nitric Oxide test strips

**Problem Indicators:** Low <50; cascading hormone & immune problems

**Frequency Protocol:** No frequency for Vitamin D → sunlight needed

**Supplementation / Interventions:** Sun exposure (primary)
10,000 IU/day × 4-6 wks; maintenance 4,000 IU
Vitamin K: 200 mcg per 1,000 IU D
Light devices: Sperti, Chroma, EMR Tek

**Source:** All 4
