# BFM Master Protocol Key: Condition-Specific Protocols

Disease and condition-specific protocol stacks with frequency combinations,
supplementation, and treatment timelines. Organized by care category
(Thyroid, Neurological, Hormones, Diabetes).

---

## Care Category: THYROID CONDITIONS

### Condition: Hashimoto's (TPO+)

**Care Category:** THYROID CONDITIONS

**Frequency Protocol(s):** Thyroid One (1x/wk); Autoimmune (other days); All deal breaker freqs

**Supplementation:** Cell Synergy; Thyroiden; X39 patch 2wk/Glutathione patch 2wk cycle over thyroid

**Timeline:** 3-6 months

**Notes:** Always check PNS negative + vagus (commonly present)

**Source Module:** Thyroid

---

### Condition: Hashimoto's + ANA positive

**Care Category:** THYROID CONDITIONS

**Frequency Protocol(s):** All above + Mito Tox); CT Repair

**Supplementation:** Add MitoKond (Innovita) if heavy mito work

**Timeline:** 3-6 months

**Notes:** Double whammy: antibiotic-induced CT damage + Hashimoto's

**Source Module:** Thyroid

---

### Condition: Goiter

**Care Category:** THYROID CONDITIONS

**Frequency Protocol(s):** Goiter → Thyroid CT (if goiter doesn't fully shrink)

**Supplementation:** Thyroiden

**Timeline:** Weeks–months

**Notes:** CT setting for connective tissue component

**Source Module:** Thyroid

### Condition: Parkinson's

**Care Category:** NEUROLOGICAL CONDITIONS

**Frequency Protocol(s):** Basal Ganglia; Solar Plexus; Vagus; Cord; Forebrain; Medulla; Midbrain; Schumann Midbrain; Cord 58 at 0.1 (tremors)

**Supplementation:** Cell Synergy; Pectasol-C; CoQ10; B6 (300mg/day with Carbidopa); MitoKond; Rejuvenation (DrinkHRW)

**Timeline:** Long-term

**Notes:** Test tremor response: Cord Stem → Brain Stem → Basal Ganglia Stem → Sensory Motor Cortex Stem

**Source Module:** Neuro

---

### Condition: Alzheimer's

**Care Category:** NEUROLOGICAL CONDITIONS

**Frequency Protocol(s):** Hippocampus; Hind Brain; Capillary/Artery/Vein support; Pineal; Brain Proteins (if galatin-3 high)

**Supplementation:** Cell Synergy (has direct research on beta-amyloid); Pectasol-C; Rejuvenation (DrinkHRW); CoQ10; PEOs; B vitamins

**Timeline:** Long-term

**Notes:** Cell Synergy research shows reduction in beta-amyloid

**Source Module:** Neuro

---

### Condition: Multiple Sclerosis

**Care Category:** NEUROLOGICAL CONDITIONS

**Frequency Protocol(s):** Cyto Lower (1hr 45min); Cord Stem; Hind Brain (60-70% of cases); Pineal (always); Pituitary (60-70%)

**Supplementation:** Cell Synergy; Pectasol-C; drainage/detox support

**Timeline:** Long-term

**Notes:** MSH unmeasurable in 60-70% of MS patients; check pineal cyst on MRI

**Source Module:** Neuro

---

### Condition: Chemo Neuropathy

**Care Category:** NEUROLOGICAL CONDITIONS

**Frequency Protocol(s):** PN Tox (most powerful)

**Supplementation:** Rejuvenation (DrinkHRW); nerve support

**Timeline:** Acute: fast; Chronic: 2 mo at 2x/wk

**Notes:** High success rate noted

**Source Module:** Neuro

### Condition: Diabetic Neuropathy

**Care Category:** NEUROLOGICAL CONDITIONS

**Frequency Protocol(s):** Nerve Pain; PN Diabetes (vascular component); PN Toxicity if not responding

**Supplementation:** Support based on comorbidities

**Timeline:** Variable

**Notes:** Vascular component is key differentiator

### Condition: Fibromyalgia

**Care Category:** NEUROLOGICAL CONDITIONS

**Frequency Protocol(s):** Copper Balance (excess copper = hallmark); Cyto Lower (sometimes CPP), Nerve Pain and Sympathetic Calm

**Supplementation:** Cell Synergy (1 scoop); Rejuvenation H2 (3 tabs/day = 11% insulin sensitivity in 28 days); Pancreos (Innovita)

**Notes:** May reverse if caught early; check C-Peptide
